The Claude Skills Playbook I Wish I Had on Day One

From: AI Maker (One Shot Show) Apr 26, 2026 Label: AI 
For Episode 7 of One Shot Show, Ilia Karelin joined Dheeraj Sharma (GenAI Unplugged) as our AI expert guest to walk through Claude Skills live. He came on to answer: 'Should I be building Claude Skills yet, and how do I know which ones are worth the effort?'

A skill is a reusable instruction set Claude follows when relevant -- a SKILL.md file and reference docs. Claude pulls it in automatically when your message matches, or you invoke it by slash command. It is an SOP that runs itself.

How to spot a skill candidate: 'If I have to repeat something like a long prompt three times a week, that is a pattern. Then I convert it into a skill.' Three times in a week is the threshold.

The Skill Creator is a meta-skill pre-installed in Claude Desktop and Claude Cowork. Invoke it: 'Help me create a [name] skill using the Skill Creator.' Building a skill takes about five minutes.

What is included: full 60-min session recording, Ilia's Fast Research skill packaged for install, and a four-element build walkthrough.
