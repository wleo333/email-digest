From: Morning Brew Apr 26, 2026 Label: News 
Unscripted shows fell 15% last year, now a third below 2022 level. HGTV: 35 shows in 2025 vs. 78 in 2019. Streaming ordered 31% fewer reality series in 2025 vs. prior year. Cable penetration now ~50% of US households (down from ~90% in 2010). YouTube accounts for ~13% of US viewing time. Twitch has 21M+ active streamers, athlete/celebrity streams up 20% YoY.

Behind the scenes: The Bachelorette season postponed indefinitely 3 days before March premiere. Love is Blind Season 10 drew manosphere accusations and a sexual assault lawsuit. America's Next Top Model Netflix documentary brings fresh criticism of Tyra Banks. Real Housewives of NYC lawsuit against Bravo and Andy Cohen proceeding in federal court.

Survivor recently premiered its 50th season (debuted 26 years ago). Love Island USA was the most-watched original streaming show last year with 18B minutes on Peacock.
